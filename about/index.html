Having been raised on both sides of the Atlantic, I grew up enthralled by the music of words. As the daughter of a translator and interpreter, I could not help but feel drawn to my mother's mysterious and scintillating world of languages - a world infused with sonorous colour, where cultural boundaries take on fluid and melodious meanings. From teaching and translation to literature and the natural world around me, this love of words has never ceased to guide me and - much as a beacon upon the shores of a turbulent sea - remains my faithful phare lighting the uncharted waters ahead.

Whether your query concerns translation, English instruction or anything in between, please do not hesitate to contact me for further information. Whilst I may be in Switzerland, I welcome clients from around the world, and I look forward to working with you in whatever way this may be.
