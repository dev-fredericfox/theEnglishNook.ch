Translation & Proofreading

French/German into English

Having been working as a volunteer translator since 2016 for the Wiener Holocaust Library, one of the largest archives dedicated to the Second World War, I have years of experience translating texts ranging from official reports and research studies to eyewitness accounts and wartime memoirs. Regardless of the material in question, my approach reflects not only the great value I place on producing fine-tuned, research-based work but also my analytical and methodical nature evincing precision, diligence and a keen awareness of nuance and subtlety. Should your query concern translation, proofreading or any work related to these fields, please do not hesitate to contact me here or at info@theenglishnook.ch. It would be my pleasure to discuss whatever projects you may have.
